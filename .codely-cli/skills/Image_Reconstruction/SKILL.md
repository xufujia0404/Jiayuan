# Unity Image Reconstruction Skills Master Reference

This directory contains expert instructions for reconstructing assets and scenes from 2D images.

## 📁 1. Image to Assets
Located in: `00_Image_to_Assets.md`
- **Focus**: Decomposing a scene image into individual sprites and 3D models.
- **Use Case**: Extracting props, characters, or environment pieces from a concept art.

## 📁 2. Image to 3D Scene
Located in: `01_Image_to_3D_Scene.md`
- **Focus**: Full 3D scene reconstruction from a single 2D viewpoint.
- **Use Case**: Turning a concept painting or a screenshot into a navigable Unity environment.

---
**Tip**: When using `Image to 3D Scene`, always double-check the **Flip-X Rule** for positions and rotations to ensure the scene isn't mirrored.
